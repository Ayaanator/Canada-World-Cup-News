On November 27, 2020, it was a historic day for the World Cup. On that day, Canada scored its first ever goal in the World Cup. It was scored by Alphonso Davies, one of the forwards. He made a header near the goal and was assisyed by Tajon Buchanan. Other forwards of the team include:
  • Jonathan David
  • Tajon Buchanan
  • Cyle Larin
  • Junior Hoilett
  • Lucas Cavallini
  • Liam Millar
Davies has brought joy to a lot of fans with that goal as all Candians cheered and screamed. Canada waited for 36 years, and Davies delivered. "I looked to my left and just saw all my teammates running toward me," said Davies, "It was a great feeling. We've been waiting for that moment for a long time."

Fastest Goal in World Cup 2022

After missing the penalty in the last Canada vs Belgium match, Alphonso proved he would do much better and redeemed himself in the next Canada vs Croatia match. He scored within the second minute of the match, at 68 seconds, making it the fastest goal ever in the 2022 World Cup. Unfortunately, Canada lost 1-4 to Croatia that match. Despite this, they also found victory within defeat. For many Canadians, that first score ever beats any defeats they have faced. Also, tt not very surprising Croatia won. Canada is a new and inexperienced team going up against a strong team that been in several world cups, and they did very well for a new team. They had 6 saves and 5 fouls, while Croatia had 1 save and 13 fouls.
